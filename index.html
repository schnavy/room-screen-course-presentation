Is it a room in a screen or a screen in a room?
Schmitt Anna-Lena
Projekt Titel Student Name
Projekt Description
Open Project ↗
A website can lie in bed on a mobile screen at night, serve as a display board at an crowded airport terminal, make executives sweat on the projector wall in the meeting room, or be part of installations or performative acts as an artistic medium. Screens and their contents always live in relation to the space/room in which they appear. Similarly, the digital space opened by a website can completely change or expand the physical space. There is a before and behind the screen – but these worlds are neither separate nor truly one. Through each interaction, a new space is created between the spaces.
